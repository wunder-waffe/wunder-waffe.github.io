Homepage

Hello, my name is Berkehan Ercan. I live in Ankara, Turkey and I am a 4th year Electrical and Electronics Engineering Student at Bilkent University. I have created this page to document my personal projects, my experiences with school and my interships. I might also post more personal things here. My projects cover a broad spectrum, from DIY 8-bit CPUs to building antennas, and from home chemistry experiments to astronomy/astrophotography. I work with anything that I find remotely interesting. The goal of this blog is to document all of these in an unfiltered manner. I have wanted to create this website for a long time and I have even registered the original domain back in April 2023 but I never got around it until now. The old domain was confusing as it was my ham callsign. It bounced web indexing services for a long time and I didn't achieve the name-website association that I wanted.
